## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior using SQL, Power BI, and business intelligence techniques to identify high-value customers, evaluate loyalty patterns, measure promotional dependency, and recommend retention strategies.

The primary objective was to help a retail brand understand which customers generate long-term value, which segments are at risk of churn, and how promotional dependency can be reduced while maintaining sustainable business growth.

---

## Business Objective

* Identify the most valuable customer segments.
* Distinguish genuinely loyal customers from promotion-driven customers.
* Evaluate factors influencing customer value and retention.
* Discover product categories that drive long-term loyalty.
* Identify geographic opportunities for growth.
* Develop actionable customer retention strategies.

## Key Findings

### 1. Largest Growth Opportunity

Potential Loyalists represent the largest customer segment, indicating significant potential for conversion into Champion customers.

### 2. Strongest Loyalty Category

Footwear demonstrates the highest customer loyalty and customer value performance among all product categories.

### 3. Retention Risk

A substantial At-Risk customer segment highlights the need for proactive retention initiatives.

### 4. Promotional Dependency

Several high-value customer groups remain highly dependent on promotions, indicating potential profitability risks if discounts are reduced without alternative engagement strategies.

### 5. Geographic Opportunities

Customer loyalty and value vary across regions, revealing opportunities for targeted market expansion and localized retention campaigns.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

* Convert Potential Loyalists into Champion customers through targeted loyalty programs.
* Increase investment in high-performing product categories such as Footwear.
* Launch retention campaigns focused on At-Risk customers.
* Reduce discount dependency through personalized engagement strategies.
* Implement region-specific initiatives to improve customer retention and market performance.
